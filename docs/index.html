Probabilistic Programming

We have provided an universal probabilistic programming with an intuitive modelling interface.

group

MCMC Samplers

Turing.jl has Hamiltonian Monte Carlo (HMC) sampling for differentiable posterior distributions and particle MCMC sampling for complex posterior distributions involving discrete variables and stochastic control flows.

settings

Compositional MCMC Interface

We have provided an interface for users to compose different samplers (e.g HMC and PG) in a Gibbs manner.



Cambridge Machine Learning Group

We are a part of the Computational and Biological Learning Laboratory located in the Department of Engineering at the University of Cambridge. The research in our group is very broad, and we are interested in all aspects of machine learning. Particular strengths of the group are in Bayesian approaches to modelling and inference in statistical applications.
